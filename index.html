What Should I Do While Waiting for a Plumber to Arrive?

If you have a leak or burst pipe, the first step is to locate and turn off your stopcock to stop the water supply. This can usually be found under the kitchen sink or near the water meter. Turn off any electrical appliances near the affected area and mop up standing water where it's safe to do so.

Keep the area accessible so the plumber can get straight to work on arrival. If it's a heating issue, note down any error codes on your boiler display, as this information can save diagnostic time.
